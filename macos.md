## Keyboard shortcuts

- Ctrl-P Move up  
- Ctrl-N Move down  
- Ctrl-B Move left  
- Ctrl-F Move right  
- Ctrl-A Go to start of paragraph  
- Ctrl-E Go to end of paragraph  
- Ctrl-D Delete forward  
- Ctrl-H Backspace  
- Ctrl-K Delete to end of paragraph  
- Ctrl-T Transpose  
- Ctrl-O Insert new line
